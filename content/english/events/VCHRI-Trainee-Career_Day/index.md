Join the Vancouver Coastal Health Research Institute (VCHRI) Trainee Committee for a full day of interactive, career-focused programming designed to strengthen your professional skills and support your next steps with confidence. 
The program exists of 2 parts: morning session are interactive workshops focused on professional development and the afternoon session will feature a panel discussion. 

The full program can be found [here](https://www.vchri.ca/sites/default/files/VCHRI%20Trainee%20Career%20Day%20Program_0.pdf).
