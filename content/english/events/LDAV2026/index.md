The Life Sciences Data Association Vancouver (LDAV) is hosting its inaugural event on Thursday 17th of June from 5pm - 8pm, co-hosted by the VPC.  This evening event will bring together professionals working at the intersection of life sciences, statistics, and data science for an hour of talks, followed by casual networking and light refreshments. More info can be found [here](https://www.prostatecentre.com/sites/default/files/imce/ldav_vpc.july17.pdf) and RSVP [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdg7fci66jdNZjfBFYkkHwBI9xW0Swvzi5kdh11fahQGnGZEg/closedform).
